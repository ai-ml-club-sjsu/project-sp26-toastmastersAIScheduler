**Academic Year:** 2025-2026 Spring Semester  
**Project Duration:** February 2026 - May 2026

## Project Description

Toastmasters meetings run on time, preparation, and people showing up ready. Yet one of the most consistent challenges across clubs is scheduling—assigning roles, handling availability, sending reminders, managing last-minute changes, and making sure everyone gets fair speaking opportunities.

**Key Objectives:**
- Automatically assign meeting roles based on availability, experience level, and past participation
- Adapt in real time to cancellations or late changes
- Ensure fair rotation of speaking and leadership roles 
- Send intelligent reminders and confirmations
- Reduce officer workload and burnout
